About us

Our Story

FitStyle was born out of a shared passion for fitness and a desire to create gym clothes that not only perform but also look great. Our founders,Quang Phat Mai, were avid athletes who were frustrated by the lack of stylish and functional workout apparel on the market. Inspired by their own experiences, they set out to design a collection that would empower individuals to reach their fitness goals while expressing their personal style. With a focus on quality, comfort, and innovation, FitStyle was founded in 2020

Our Values

At FitStyle, we believe in the power of fitness to transform lives. Our core values guide our actions and decisions:

  1. Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality gym clothes, using premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship.
  2. Innovation: We strive to be at the forefront of fitness fashion, constantly seeking new and innovative designs.
  3. Sustainability: We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in our operations.
  4. Community: We believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive community of fitness enthusiasts.

Our team

Our team is made up of passionate fitness professionals and industry experts who share a common goal: to help people achieve their fitness dreams. From our designers to our customer service representatives, everyone at FitStyle is dedicated to providing exceptional service and products.

Sustainability

At FitStyle, we believe that sustainability is essential for a healthy planet and a thriving future. We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting eco-friendly practices in all aspects of our business.

Sustainable Materials
We source high-quality, sustainable materials for our gym clothes. This includes recycled fabrics, organic cotton, and biodegradable fibers.
Ethical Production
We work with ethical manufacturers who prioritize fair labor practices and environmental responsibility.
Reduced Waste
We strive to minimize waste throughout our production process, from material sourcing to packaging.
Carbon Footprint Reduction
We are actively working to reduce our carbon footprint by implementing energy-efficient practices and minimizing transportation emissions.
Community Partnerships
We support organizations that promote sustainability and environmental conservation.

Community Involvement

We believe in giving back to the community and supporting local fitness initiatives. We partner with various organizations to promote healthy living, organize fitness events, and provide opportunities for people to connect and inspire each other.
